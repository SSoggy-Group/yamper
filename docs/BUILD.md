# Build notes

## Mechanical layout

- Back opens with 4 M3 screws.
- Raspberry Pi Zero 2 WH and PiSugar sit on the back cover.
- OLEDs mount behind the two front eye windows.
- Speaker sits behind the smaller front grille.
- Mic sits behind the small round hole pattern.
- MAX98357A sits in the side pocket.
- Retainers stop the parts from falling inward.

## First print checklist

Print only the front shell first.

Test these parts:

- OLED board fits in the eye pockets
- speaker fits in the 25 x 35 mm pocket
- mic fits the 14 mm holder
- 16 mm button fits the top hole
- screw posts do not look weak

## Do not trust the full print yet

This version is a beta fit model. If a part is off by 1 to 2 mm, update the CAD before printing the final clean version.
