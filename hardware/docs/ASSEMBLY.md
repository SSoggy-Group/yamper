# Assembly

## Front shell

1. Place the two OLED screens behind the eye openings.
2. Use the OLED retainers to stop the OLEDs from falling inward.
3. Place the 25 x 35 mm speaker behind the speaker grille.
4. Use the speaker retainer to hold the speaker.
5. Place the INMP441 microphone behind the five mic holes.
6. Use the round mic retainer to hold the mic module.
7. Place the MAX98357A amplifier in its clip.
8. Install the 16 mm button in the top hole.

## Back cover

1. Mount the Raspberry Pi Zero 2 WH and PiSugar stack to the back cover standoffs.
2. Check that the PiSugar button lines up with the long power slot.
3. Use the plunger only after checking alignment with the real board.

## Closing the case

1. Put the back cover on the front shell.
2. Insert M3 self-tapping screws from the outside of the back cover.
3. The screws pass through the back cover and bite into the screw bosses in the front shell.
4. Do not overtighten.

Do not screw into or through the battery cell.
