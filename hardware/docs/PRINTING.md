# Printing

## Front shell

- Front face flat on the bed
- Inside facing upward
- Layer height: 0.20 mm
- Wall loops: 3
- Infill: 15%
- Supports: off first

## Back cover

- Outside face flat on the bed
- Standoffs facing upward
- Layer height: 0.20 mm
- Wall loops: 3
- Infill: 15%

## Small parts

Print the retainers flat.
